About the Social Group

Our social group's goal is simple. To create lasting friendships with other neurodivergent individuals. We do this by hosting monthly meet ups. We hope to see you there!

How we got started

After graduating high school, I realized that making friends as a neurodivergent was more difficult. During my time in college, I was inspired too bring my friends together, creating this social group. My goal is to help other neurodivergent individuals find and create friendships.
